Article 1 Going Solar: Common Questions and Concerns Addressed

JANUARY 17, 2019 admin 3 COMMENTS

Solar energy is becoming increasingly popular as people look for ways to reduce their carbon footprint and save money on their energy bills. However, many people still have questions and concerns about switching to solar power. In this article, we will address some of the most common questions and concerns about going solar.

How much will it cost to install solar panels?

The cost of installing solar panels depends on several factors, such as the size of your home or business, your energy usage, and the type of solar panels you choose. However, there are often incentives available from the government, such as tax credits and rebates, that can significantly reduce the cost of installation. Additionally, many solar companies offer financing options, making it easier to afford the upfront cost of installation.

Will solar panels work in my climate?

Solar panels can work in most climates, including cloudy and overcast weather. However, solar panels are most effective when they receive direct sunlight. Therefore, it's important to ensure that your roof or property receives adequate sunlight to make the switch to solar power worthwhile.

Will solar panels increase the value of my home?

Yes, solar panels can increase the value of your home. According to a study conducted by the Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, homes with solar panels sold for an average of $15,000 more than homes without solar panels.

How long do solar panels last?

Solar panels can last up to 25-30 years or even longer with proper maintenance. Additionally, most solar companies offer warranties on their panels, ensuring that any issues are addressed promptly.

What happens if there is a power outage?

Most solar systems are designed to automatically shut off in the event of a power outage to protect workers who may be repairing the grid. However, some solar systems have a battery backup that allows homeowners to continue using electricity during a power outage.

What happens if I move?

If you move, you can either transfer the solar panel system to the new owner or sell the system separately. Solar panels can increase the value of your home and make it more attractive to potential buyers.

Will solar panels require maintenance?

Solar panels require minimal maintenance, such as keeping them clean and free of debris. Most solar companies offer maintenance services to ensure that your solar panel system is working efficiently.

In conclusion, switching to solar power can be a great way to reduce your carbon footprint and save money on your energy bills. While there are some upfront costs and concerns to consider, the benefits of going solar often outweigh the drawbacks. If you have more questions or concerns, it's best to speak with a professional solar company to learn more about how solar panels can work for you.
