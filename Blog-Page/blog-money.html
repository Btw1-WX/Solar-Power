How to Earn Money from Excess Solar Power

JANUARY 17, 2019 admin 3 COMMENTS

Solar power is becoming a popular source of renewable energy for many households and businesses. As solar panels generate a lot of energy during the day, sometimes the amount produced may exceed the demand. In such situations, solar panel owners have the opportunity to earn money from their excess energy.

There are several ways to earn money from excess solar power, including net metering, feed-in tariffs, virtual power plants, and peer-to-peer trading. Net metering allows solar panel owners to sell the excess energy they generate back to the grid, while feed-in tariffs provide payments to solar panel owners for the excess energy they produce.


Virtual power plants are a network of solar panels and batteries that can sell excess energy back to the grid, and peer-to-peer trading enables solar panel owners to sell their excess energy directly to other consumers. As solar power continues to become more prevalent, we can expect to see more opportunities for solar panel owners to earn money from their excess energy in the future.
